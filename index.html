About Us

The SJCET Startup Bootcamp- IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre)

was set up on 9th March 2015 as a part of the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) initiative to develop a startup culture amongst students. The IEDCs are platforms set up in Engineering, Management, Arts & Science Colleges and Polytechnics with an aim to provide students an opportunity to experiment and innovate. KSUM has set up IEDCs in 226 institutions across the State which provide avenues for creative students to learn, collaborate and transform their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products and services. IEDCs works as the first launch pad for a student’s entrepreneurial journey and provide them with access to cutting edge technology, world class infrastructure, high quality mentorship, early risk capital and global exposure. With the various ideation and problem solving maker events conducted by the Bootcamp, students are encouraged to challenge themselves and come up with innovative ideas that. Students are provided with expert mentorship to convert their ideas into prototypes, and eventually into marketable products. Infusory Future Tech Labs Pvt Ltd ( graduated in 2018) and Lamaara Technologies Pvt Ltd (graduated in 2019) are two startup companies founded by the students of SJCET Palai and incubated in the SJCET Startup Bootcamp. During their incubation period, Lamaara Technologies Pvt Ltd received a brand equity investment worth 50 lakh Rs from the Times Group. SJCET Bootcamp received the Best Performing IEDC award in 2017 and 2019. In 2019, Bootcamp conducted the Startup Yathra and also received the Hero of the Startup award, the best sustainable startup award, and the best women wannapreneur award. In the Smart India Hackathon 2019, organised by MHRD and AICTE , the product ‘Blackbox for drones’ from Bootcamp received the best innovative product award. In 2020, SJCET Startup Bootcamp hosted the NASA Space Apps Challenge and gained recognition all over the nation. Other major achievements are: NASSCOM STARTUP 20-20 winner, 2015. Team PENTA from SJCET Palai won the first prize worth 1 lakh Rs at the ICTT ECHATHLON organised by the ICT Academy of Kerala in 2016.

Our Motive

Lets Get Your Dreams Incubated

IDEA

Every little thing comes from an idea. Ideation is all about coming up with possible solutins to problems that hinder us in our daily lives. But every idea requires proper guidance and modulation so as to raise the best solution possible. SJCET Bootcamp identifies the best idea from the root, encourages young minds to formulate them and show them the correct path to make it stand high.

INCUBATE

The process of bringing those ideas into the reality is the idea incubation. Once an idea is pitched, the primary foundation is proper incubation.With collection of resources, teaming up with mentors, organizing ideathons, the Maker Space of SJCET IEDC stands tall as the perfect incubation centre.

PROTOTYPE

Every entrepreneurial journey has the final goal of bringing the idea to the business platform. Professional approach along with technological support is needed for this not-so-easy pathway. The preliminary stage involves the prototype phase where every product is evaluated and then deployed. SJCET Bootcamp guides through this phase and you meet your dream.
